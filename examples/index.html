JS Int-Range Utility Function

Basic

Default
intRange({ start: 1, limit: 5 });
Specify Spacing
intRange({ start: 1, limit: 15, sequence: 3 });
Increment || Decrement!
intRange({ start: 25, limit: 1, sequence: 5 });

Advanced

Even
intRange({ start: 1, limit: 20, sequence: even({ steps: 2 }) });
Odd
intRange({ start: 1, limit: 20, sequence: odd({ steps: 2 }) });
Multiples
intRange({ start: 1, limit: 35, sequence: multiples({ of: 5, steps: 2 }) });
Custom [& Pluggable] Sequences
intRange({ start: 1980, limit: 2020, sequence: leapYear({ steps: 2 }) });
